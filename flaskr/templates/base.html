About Hydropatrol

Hydropatrol is a platform enabling monitoring and prediction of algal bloom. Our mission is to fight a global problem towards safe and sustainable future. We use space data to save lifes.

The Problem

Harmful Algal Bloom is a rapid increase of algae population in freshwater or marine water systems. It is a global problem which costs millions USD annualy. It leads to dramatic consequences, such as:

  • Increased risk of cancer or liver failure which can threaten human life
  • Death of animals due to intoxicated water
  • Drinking water shortage
  • Economical loss for fishing industry
  • Decrease of value for lakeside properties
  • Forbidden swimming/boating
  • Millions USD spent annualy for algal bloom prevention and treatment

350 elephants dead due to toxins in watering holes in Botswana (2020)
500'000 people with 'NO WATER' supply for 3 days in Toledo, USA (2014)

Our Solution

Algal bloom is a complex global problem. Global problems are not solvable by singular players. Global problems are not solvable by acting locally. Our platform ensures we get to the cost-effective algal bloom solution by:

Our platform enables mutual collaboration and data sharing among all actors in the system, including: space data providers, research institutions, government organizations, fisching associations, touristic resorts, education facilities and many more.
Our Machine Learning model uses and online monitoring water, sending you warnings and preventing costs.
We use custom-built IoT devices for getting in-situ measurements without cost and time waste. This ensures our database is continually enlarged and the model improves.
Thanks to extensive multi-actor collaboration, we build a global database of in-situ measurements. We add the domain expertise of our research parners as well as the Copernicus space data. This enables us to create a non-local predictive model which achieves high results despite the complexity of the problem.
While prevention and counteracting are important, we also enable technology to turn the problem into value. We build technology for turning algal waste into biofuel, thus eliminate the problem and work towards sustainability at the same time.
